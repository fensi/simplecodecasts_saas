Management Profile

DR. SAMUEL AMOAKO

Dr. Samuel Amoako, is a Director, and the Chief Executive Officer of ASAMAY CONSULT LIMITED. He is an astute Chartered Certified Accountant with the requisite academic and professional qualifications and a professional track record. He has over 15 years extensive practical experience in the areas of Accounting and Finance, Operations Management, Business Development Services, Business valuation and Investment, Business Plans and Human Resources Development and Management from consulting engagements, commerce and industries.

He is the Trainer and Facilitator for the Associates and Full Membership of the Chartered Institute of Financial and Investment Analysts, Ghana. A Lecturer in Financial Management at Elexxon Business and Financial Institute-Accra, Hent Financial Training Centre-Accra, and Suncity Study Centre-Sunyani.

He is an independent expert specializing in consultancy on clean, renewable and sustainable energy for Climate Technology Initiative – Private Financing Advisory Network (CTI-PFAN). (http://cti-pfan.net/about/#a_membership), undertaking coaching and mentoring on Business and Strategic planning, and conducting independent feasibility audits on projects.

He holds a Doctorate degree in Business Administration with a major in Business Administration, a Master of Business Administration degree in Accounting and Finance, Bachelor of Science Degree in Accounting, Post-graduate Diploma in Project Management and a Diploma in Accountancy. He is a Fellow of Association of Chartered Certified Accountants-UK, Fellow of Chartered Institute of Financial and Investment Analysts-Ghana, a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants-Ghana, Chartered Global Investment Analyst-USA and a Certified Project Management Consultant from the Institute of Project Management Professionals.

He is a good team player with a wealth of professional initiatives and ideas. He has excellent attributes in interpersonal skills, influencing skills and an insatiable taste for results and achievement.

MARGARET ASUG-BEY YAKUBU

Margaret Asug-Bey Yakubu is a Director, Business Advisory and Consulting Services of ASAMAY CONSULT LIMITED. She also works at BGJ as the Audit Associates and Administrative Manager. She has over 5 years extensive practical experience in the areas of Auditing and Assurance Services, Accounting and Finance, Operations Management, Business Development Services, and Management from commerce, construction, mining and industries.

She holds a Bachelor Degree in Business and Commerce and a Higher National Diploma in Accountancy. She is a student member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants-Ghana and currently at level 3.

Margaret is very sociable and as such a good mixer. She is a very good team player though an independent minded person and exhibits a high sense of responsibility.
